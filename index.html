Research

I aim to design self-supervised models that maximally and efficiently utilize the supervision from different types of datasets (visual-spatial, symbolic-combinatory) and build more generalizable AI models.
I'm also interested in the interplay between language and vision: compressing visual scenes using texts and generating images from textual inputs.
My past and current research areas of focus include self-supervised learning, visual reasoning, and generative modeling.
